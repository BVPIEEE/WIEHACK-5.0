WIEHack 5.0

Presented by IEEE BVCOE

WIEHack 5.0, the fifth iteration of IEEE BVCOE's flagship event, builds on the legacy of empowering women in technology since 2018. This 36-hour Women-Centric Hybrid International Hackathon is designed to give your ideas the time they deserve, encouraging collaboration and learning among participants. WIEHack aims to address gender disparity in the tech world by requiring that each team have at least 50% female members, thus creating a supportive environment where ideas can thrive and voices can be heard distinctly. We believe innovation is fueled by diverse perspectives, which is why WIEHack adopts a hybrid format, welcoming both international and local participants. Whether you join us virtually or in person, WIEHack offers an immersive experience that promotes collaboration, innovation, and skill display.

FAQ

Teams for WIEHack 5.0 can have 1-4 members and male students can also be part of the team provided minimum 50% are female members in a team.

To register for the offline mode, participants can choose the option during registration on Unstop. However, please note that only 70 teams will be accommodated offline on a first-come, first-served basis. The remaining teams are encouraged to join us through the online mode.

No, participants will be responsible for their own travel expenses to attend offline.

Online participation will be facilitated via Discord. A link to the Discord server will be shared a few days prior to the event, providing a platform for collaboration, communication, and support throughout the hackathon.

The event will have four rounds : Abstract Round, Prototype & Presentation Round, Business Plan Round and Final Round.

There are total of 8 themes. Teams can choose any of these themes :
  • Women Safety
  • Climate Change and Sustainability
  • Health and fitness
  • FinTech
  • Education and Skill Development
  • Cybersecurity
  • Viksit Bharat @2047: Building the solid foundation of a developed India
  • Open innovation

There is no restriction on the programming languages and platforms used to develop the product.

Participating in WIEHack 5.0 offers networking opportunities with industry experts and peers, exciting goodies, certificates of participation, and the chance to win cash prizes for the winners.
